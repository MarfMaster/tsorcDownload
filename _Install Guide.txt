ESTIMATED TIME: 3 MINUTES

1. Download tModLoader from Steam: https://store.steampowered.com/app/1281930/tModLoader/

2. Open tModLoader and select Mod Browser, then search for The Story of Red Cloud or tsorc. Download and load the TSORC Revamp mod. 

3. Copy "The Story of Red Cloud.wld" to Documents\My Games\Terraria\ModLoader\Worlds

4. Copy "tsorcMusic.tmod" to Documents\My Games\Terraria\ModLoader\Mods

5. Open tModLoader and ensure the mods are Enabled via the "Mods" menu.

Done!
